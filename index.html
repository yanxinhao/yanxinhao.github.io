Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at ShanghaiTech University, jointly supervised by Prof. Shenghua Gao (The University of Hong Kong) and Prof. Lan Xu (ShanghaiTech University).

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics and vision, with a focus on 3D representation learning and generative models. Recently, I have been exploring 3D scene-level generative models that can benefit spatial intelligence and embodied AI. I am passionate about new ideas and collaborations.
